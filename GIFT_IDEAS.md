## üí° Tips for Long-Distance Gifts

### Budget-Friendly Options
- Mix expensive and inexpensive gifts
- Many digital gifts are free or low-cost
- Handmade items are priceless

### Timing Considerations
- Ship physical gifts in advance
- Schedule digital gifts to arrive on the right day
- Consider time zones for virtual experiences

### Personalization is Key
- Add personal notes to everything
- Reference inside jokes or memories
- Show you know her interests and preferences

### Make It Interactive
- Plan activities you can do together
- Create shared experiences
- Build anticipation for future visits
